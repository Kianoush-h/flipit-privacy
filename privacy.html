Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

FlipIt ("we", "our", or "the app") is a social betting application where friends wager on real-life events using an in-app virtual wallet. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use FlipIt, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address, username, and display name provided during registration.
  • Betting activity: Bets created, accepted, and settled within rooms, including stakes, choices, and outcomes.
  • Wallet data: Virtual wallet balance and transaction history within the app.
  • Room data: Rooms you create or join, and associated membership information.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FlipIt app and its features.
  • Manage your account, wallet, and betting activity.
  • Enable room creation, invitations, and real-time notifications.
  • Communicate with you about your account or app updates.
  • Ensure fair play and resolve disputes between users.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted backend platform with row-level security and encryption. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

FlipIt uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication, database, and real-time functionality.
  • Expo: App framework and build services.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Bluetooth and Camera Permissions

FlipIt may request access to:

  • Camera: Used solely for scanning QR codes to join rooms. No images are stored or transmitted.
  • Bluetooth: Used solely for discovering nearby FlipIt rooms. No personal data is broadcast or collected via Bluetooth.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your betting activity and wallet balance are visible only to members of rooms you have joined. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and betting data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Bet history may be retained in anonymized form for record-keeping purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

FlipIt is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
